About

Pallet Loading Optimizer is a free browser-based tool that calculates how many cartons fit on a pallet, visualizes the stacking pattern in 3D, and reports pallet area occupancy so you can compare loading patterns before you ship. It supports T11, Euro/EPAL, US GMA, ISO and Australian pallet standards, gap clearance, interlocked vs. column stacking, and exports a printable PDF report — all directly in your browser, with no installation or account required.

This site is built and maintained independently by a developer focused on free, practical logistics and packaging tools. It is not affiliated with any pallet manufacturer or standards body. If you have questions, feedback, or found a calculation that looks wrong, see the Contact page.
