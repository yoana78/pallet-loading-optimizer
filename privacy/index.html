Overview

Pallet Loading Optimizer (“we”, “us”, “this site”) is a free browser-based calculator available at https://pallet-loading-layout-optimizer.pages.dev. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the site and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Pallet Loading Optimizer does not require an account and does not ask you to submit personal information to use the calculator. Any dimensions, weights, or other values you enter are processed locally in your browser and are not transmitted to or stored on our servers. If you contact us by email, we receive whatever information you choose to include in that message (see Contact).

Third-Party Vendors

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site. This may include vendors delivering ads through the Google AdSense program.

Your Choices

Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect ad personalization but will not prevent you from using the calculator itself, since all calculations run locally in your browser.
